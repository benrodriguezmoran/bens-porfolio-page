Projects

Electric Vectoring "Rocket-like" Drone

Two 8 inch 3 blade propellers and a servo controlled gimbal, the goal is to build a system to debug and tune inertial measurement systems for anticipated model rocket launches, and fly itself. The platform will let me use a radio control to fly this as you would a traditional quadracopter, with some caviats, or fly a preprogrammed trajectory mimicking one of a model rocket launch.

Teensy 3.2 Avionics Unit

This is the brain of the previously featured drone, it utilizes a Teensy 3.2 MCU paired with an accelerometer and barometer to interpolate the current trajectory and cross check with our altitude pressure reading. The control flow first obtains a gravity aligned inertial reference, and intergrates to determine its position. It will take both radio inputs and fly preplanned trajectories.

Spacegame in Godot Engine
