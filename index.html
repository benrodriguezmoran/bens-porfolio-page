About Me

Hello! My name is Benjamin Rodriguez-Moran - just Ben Rodriguez if that's too much - and this is my portfolio page. Here you'll get to learn a little about me, my hobbies, life, and my work. I've had a passion for understanding the mechanics of any kind of thing for as long as I can remember - I was very fond of Lego growing up. By the time I was gifted my first 3D printer, I already dove deep into CAD and design principles and was ready to apply it. Now I've brushed up on a number of diciplines when designing; one of my projects in particular necesitated me to learn C and solder up an avionics computer with a micro controller. Simply, I just like to push my knowledge and find what I learn on the way. I am also a prominent space enthusiast, the video game Kerbal Space Program is a large influence to that, as well as my passion for game design. The pictures beside in order are: me and my lovely girlfriend Anna, my dog Cherry with my cat Mochi outside, my other cat Luna with Cherry, and a screenshot of a spacecraft I made in Kerbal Space Program.

Projects

Electric Vectoring "Rocket-like" Drone

Two 8 inch 3 blade propellers and a servo controlled gimbal, the goal is to build a system to debug and tune inertial measurement systems for anticipated model rocket launches, and fly itself. The platform will let me use a radio control to fly this as you would a traditional quadracopter, with some caviats, or fly a preprogrammed trajectory mimicking one of a model rocket launch.
